Instant Meals

Instant meals is a responsive web app that is geared towards users who don't have time to cook but want home cooked meals with the use of an Instant Pot or the like.

Hypothesis:

sers are interested in a recipe app specifically designed for cooking with a pressure cooker / instant pot. Recipes are simple with easy to find ingredients, which can be filtered to include or omit certain ingredients. Users are interested in great tasting meals that require low effort / cooking skill as well as quick cooking times.

M.P.V. Objectives

  • To provide users with simple and easy recipes they can make with their instant pot/pressure cooker.
  • To allow users with minimal time or cooking skill to make great tasting home cooked meals.
  • To provide users an option to create home cooked meals using a non-traditional, low effort method of cooking.

User Research

Who are you designing for?

Individuals who what to make a meal at home but don't have time to prep & cook a proper meal or don't have the great cooking skill.

When will your audience be engaging with your product?

Users can use the app when needing a recipe for their next meal. Users can also use the app when meal planning.

Why is your audience choosing to use what you've designed and what drives this behavior?

Users are in search of convenient app that provides simple recipes and ingredients for cooking with a instant pot/pressure cooker. Users are wanting a more hands off approach to making meals while still having control of ingredients and dietary needs.

What kinds of tasks and goals will your audience be accomplishing as the use your website or app?

Create great tasting dishes using simple ingredients and easy to follow instant pot/pressure cooker recipes. Be able to filter by ingredients they have/want to use, cook time or difficulty.

Where will your audience be using what you're designing?

Users will most likely be using the app at home when searching for a recipe or making a meal. Users can also use the app at the grocery store when shopping for ingredients.

How will your user accomplish their goals?

Users will be able to accomplish their goals by providing simple recipes that can be filtered to ingredients they already have at home or to meet dietary needs.

Usability Testing

Goal

The goal of this test is to assess the learnability for users interacting with the recipe application that helps users create great tasting meals using just an instant pot. I would like to observe and measure if new users understand the interface, and how to complete basic initial functions such as logging in and finding/filtering for different types of recipes.

Objectives

  • Determine how easily users can navigate to the Recipes page.
  • Determine if users can search for different types of recipes.
  • Determine how easily users can use filters to search for recipes.
  • Gauge how intuitive the app is to navigate
  • Collect feedback about the overall usability of the app.

Tasks

  1. Pretend you already have an account for the app. How would you login?
  2. Since this is a recipe app, please navigate to the Recipes page.
  3. You have ground beef at home that you want to use, please browse for a recipe that you like with ground beef being an ingredient.
  4. You just come home from work and are super hungry. You want to make a meal that is quick and easy, how would you go about finding a recipe that fits your needs?

User Errors & Issues

  • Home screen is not intuitive enough to navigate to Recipe page.
  • Filter button needs to be more clear and noticeable.
  • Search bar should be able to filter recipes with keywords and no need for filter button.
  • Users using the navigation bar should be able to access the search function.
