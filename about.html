About

My Story

Hi, my name is Adrian Burlaza and I am a visual designer through and through. I have been a graphic designer for the past 11 years, getting my BFA in Graphic Design from SDSU. I am fluent in design concepts such as typography, color, space, hierarchy, vector illustration and audience. I also have extensive experience with photography as this is my hobby turned “job” that I do in my spare time. More recently I decided to extend my visual tool box by diving head first into UI design. With digital platforms rapidly growing I felt the need to take part in the tech world and received my certificate of completion in UI Design from CareerFoundry. With the added skills of app design, wireframing and prototyping, I feel confident in creating impactful design and bringing digital products to life.

When Im not working I am obsessed with golf, I love to play video games with the squad and Im an avid watcher of YouTube. I also love hanging out with my wife and baby girl, doing all the girl dad things.
